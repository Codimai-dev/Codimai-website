FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do you offer special pricing for startups?

Yes! We offer up to 30% discount for early-stage startups and non-profits. Contact our sales team to learn more about our startup program and available discounts.

Can I integrate my own AI models?

Absolutely! We support integration with popular AI models including OpenAI GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and custom models. Enterprise plans include full API access for custom integrations.

How secure is my data?

Your data security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II compliance, and never train our models on your data. All data is stored securely in Indian data centers with GDPR compliance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), UPI, net banking, and wire transfers for enterprise clients. All plans can be billed monthly or annually (with 20% savings).

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change your plan anytime from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle with prorated refunds. No questions asked!

What kind of support is included?

All plans include email support (24-48hr response). Professional plans get priority support (12hr response). Business plans get dedicated support (4hr response). Enterprise includes 24/7 support with dedicated account management.
